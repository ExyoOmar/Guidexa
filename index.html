The Crowded Commute

As you move through a station, Guidexa filters out static walls and focuses on moving obstacles. A soft pulse on your right shoulder tells you someone is passing by, allowing you to maintain your stride without a pause.

The Invisible Hazard

Traditional canes miss what's at chest height. Guidexa’s LiDAR "sees" the low-hanging tree branch or the open cabinet door, providing a high-intensity warning before contact occurs.

Vision & Sensing
VL53L1X ToF Sensor Array

Four Solid-state pulse-coded ToF sensors with 5-meter range. 270° horizontal field of view.

ESP-32 S3

High-Quality Microcontroller capable of delivering accurate alerts

Spatial Mapping

Dedicated Neural Engine for real-time obstacle classification and trajectory prediction.

Haptics & Feedback
Guidexa Engine 1.0

4 Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs) positioned at high-density nerve points across the shoulders and chest. Provides variable-frequency directional pulses.

Audio Integration

Bluetooth 5.3 support for optional bone-conduction audio cues via compatible headsets.
