Built-in Safety. Always On.

Guidexa goes beyond navigation — it includes thoughtful safety features to protect you in unexpected situations. From emergency alerts to intelligent fail-safes, your independence comes with peace of mind.

SOS Emergency Mode

In case of distress — a fall, getting lost, sudden disorientation, or any emergency — activate SOS with a simple double-tap or long-press gesture on the vest controls.

  • • Instantly sends your GPS location to pre-set emergency contacts (family, friends, or local services)
  • • Triggers high-intensity haptic pulses + optional loud audio alert (if connected to Bluetooth earbuds) to draw nearby help
  • • Can auto-activate on severe fall detection (via built-in accelerometer) if enabled

Intelligent Fail-Safes

Guidexa is engineered to keep working — or gracefully degrade — even if something goes wrong.

  • • Low battery warning: Gradual haptic slowdown + persistent shoulder pulses 30 minutes before shutdown
  • • Sensor failure: Switches to safe mode (reduced scanning + stronger default center pulses) and alerts user via vibration pattern
  • • Overheating / error detection: Auto-shutdown with clear haptic "stop" sequence + LED indicator (if equipped)
  • • Offline fallback: Continues basic directional haptics using last-known calibration even without full processing

Everyday Peace of Mind

These features are always active — no extra setup needed — because safety shouldn't be optional.

  • • Moving obstacle priority: Stronger pulses for fast-approaching people/vehicles
  • • Customizable intensity: Adjust via simple controls for different environments
  • • Future-proof: OTA updates for new safety patterns and features

How Guidexa Works.

Four sensors quietly scan. Your body feels the world. You move with confidence — no sound, no holding anything.

1

Scan the Space

Four VL53L1X Time-of-Flight (ToF) sensors — borrowed from self-driving car tech — pulse invisible infrared light up to 5 meters ahead and around you in a 270° field.

2

Understand the Scene

The ESP32-S3 microcontroller processes the data in real time. It distinguishes walls, stairs, fast-moving people, and head-height hazards like branches or signs.

3

Feel the Guidance

Four Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs) on your chest and shoulders create gentle, directional vibrations. A soft pulse on the right shoulder means “adjust right.” Stronger pulses warn of urgent hazards ahead.

4

Move Freely

Your intuition takes over. Hands stay free for bags, phones, or holding hands. No audio overload in noisy places. Just quiet, natural guidance — all day on one charge.
