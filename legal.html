Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

At Guidexa, we believe privacy is a fundamental human right. Our commitment to your safety includes the security of your personal data.

Data Sovereignty

Our vests process spatial data locally. This means the 360° environment mapping happens on the device hardware itself—not on our servers. We do not track your location history, nor do we sell your movement patterns to third parties.

Information We Collect

  • Order Data: Name, Email, and physical address required to fulfill your preorder.
  • Telemetry: Optional, anonymized crash reports to help our student engineers improve haptic response times.

Terms of Sale

By placing an order for a Guidexa Vest, you agree to the following terms governing the "Pre-order" phase of our student-led initiative.

Acceptance

Orders placed today are for the Summer 2026 production run. While we aim for June 2026 delivery, these dates are estimates based on our current laboratory testing schedule.

Device Usage

The Guidexa Vest is a navigational aid. It is designed to enhance spatial awareness but should not replace existing mobility tools (such as a white cane or guide dog) or personal judgment.
