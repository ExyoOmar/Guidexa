Guidexa Education Back to Product

A Student-Led Initiative

Empowering the next
generation of innovators.

Guidexa is not just a product; it’s a living laboratory. Founded by students at Al-Israa Private Secondary School, we bridge the gap between classroom theory and humanitarian impact.

Engineering Skills

Beyond the Textbook.

Our student engineers work with ESP-32 processors, ToF sensors, and haptic systems. They learn to prototype, debug, and refine hardware in the same way professional R&D departments do.

Leadership

Future Leaders.

Students manage HR, Finance, and Marketing departments, learning to lead teams and present ideas to national judges and media.

Professionalism

Corporate Discipline.

We utilize digital systems like Notion for task tracking and cloud archiving, teaching students the organizational workflows used by modern tech giants.

Humanitarian Impact

Empathy through Research.

By engaging directly with visually impaired societies in Tulkarm, students learn that technology is most powerful when it solves real human challenges.

Partnered with Injaz.

As part of the Injaz Palestine "Company Program," we have been mentored by industry experts, transforming our classroom project into a data-backed social enterprise.
