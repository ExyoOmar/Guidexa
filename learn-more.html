Deep Dive

Technology that feels human

Guidexa combines cutting-edge sensing with intuitive feedback to create guidance that adapts to you and your environment — naturally and discreetly.

Core Technology

Built on three pillars

Every part of Guidexa is designed to work seamlessly together.

Advanced Sensing

Multi-modal sensors continuously map surroundings in real time using vision, depth, and motion data — without relying on GPS or internet.

Intelligent Processing

On-device AI interprets context, predicts paths, and prioritizes relevant obstacles — all while preserving privacy.

Natural Feedback

Subtle haptic patterns and spatial audio guide you intuitively, like a gentle hand on your shoulder.

Impact

Independence changes everything

For millions of people, simple daily movement can feel uncertain or restricted. Guidexa aims to remove those barriers — not by replacing sight, but by restoring confidence.

Whether crossing a busy street, exploring a new place, or simply walking home at night — Guidexa is there when you need it, silent when you don’t.

About Us

Born from passion and purpose

We’re a student-led team driven by the belief that technology should empower, not overwhelm.

Guidexa began as a passionate school project by a team of dedicated students who wanted to make a real difference.

What started in the classroom has grown into an innovative solution with the potential to help millions regain confidence and independence.

Today, we’re developing prototypes, collecting valuable feedback, and getting ready to take Guidexa to the next level.
